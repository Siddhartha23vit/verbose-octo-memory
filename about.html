About VIT-AP University

Est 1984

Our Story

With a rich legacy of 39 years of innovation in education and research, VIT has established itself as a leader in delivering quality education. Consistently ranked among the top educational institutions in the country, the VIT group takes pride in achieving new heights of knowledge and excellence through hard work and dedication. Continuing this tradition, the pioneering leadership at VIT-AP University fosters strong academia-industry partnerships, driving forward with decisiveness and dynamism to become a leading university. VIT-AP University offers a wide range of opportunities and training programs to help students explore their abilities, develop core competencies, and undergo a holistic transformation to become better global citizens, embodying the motto 'Apply Knowledge - Improve Life.'

About the Department of Physics

The Department of Physics, established in 2017 with an initial faculty of five, has grown significantly and now boasts 25 highly qualified physicists with well-established research track records. We offer an M.Sc. Physics program, including a specialization in Energy Science and Technology, as well as a Ph.D. program with a three-year fellowship. Additionally, we provide financial support to our scholars, including a contingency fund, open-access publication fees, and travel expenses for presenting papers at international conferences.

The School of Advanced Sciences aims to become a Centre of Excellence in Education, Research, Innovation, and Entrepreneurship, generating a transformative impact on society.

About i-ReAD MAGMA 24'

The i-ReAD MAGMA 24 conference aims to bring together researchers, scientists, and industry professionals working in the field of magnetism and magnetic materials. Participants will have the opportunity to exchange knowledge, discuss recent developments, and explore collaborative opportunities. The conference spans various domains, making it relevant for undergraduates, postgraduates, Ph.D. scholars, and faculty members alike.
